Episode IV: The Dev Awakens

The Clone Logs

Not so long ago, in a galaxy far, far away...

A young AutoCAD warrior navigated the vast universe of interior construction...

As his wife braved the treacherous trials of Law School, he began to question his path among the stars.

A disturbance in the Force led him to seek a new destiny — one written in code.

His early training was scattered. Progress was slow. The Force was not yet strong with him.

But the call of the dark side of comfort would not prevail.

He made the bold decision to fully commit — to leave the old world behind and embrace a new order.

He enrolled in the Jedi Academy — a grueling 6-month coding bootcamp that tested his very resolve.

Through trials and tribulations, knowledge and power were forged within him.

His dedication did not go unnoticed. He was granted the rank of Software Engineer Intern.

But the mission was not yet complete. The galaxy of Tech held a greater calling.

Then, like a beacon through the stars, an opportunity emerged — a chance to join the ranks of Power.

He applied. He was chosen. He became a Ninja.

At Power, his skills in the realm of quality assurance grew stronger, and his command of the code deepened.

Each mission brought new wisdom. Each challenge forged a stronger warrior.

Now, the time has come to take the final step toward his true destiny in development.

The PCA and its apprenticeship — the last threshold between a Padawan and a full-fledged developer.

The rewards are great. The sacrifices, greater still.

But the Force has always been with him.

And his story is only just beginning...
